Booking Process

If you wish to attend a Luncheon, you must book a place through Fraser McFarlane or Calum Lowe. All that is required is a simple message stating your interest. Your request will then be verified by the Central Committee of the Grand Duchy of Fairfax.

If you are successful in gaining a place at the event, you will be issued with a digital ticket. You must keep this ticket safe, as it will grant you entry to the event. If you are not successful in gaining a place to the event, you will not receive a digital ticket and therefore will not be permitted entry to the event.

The booking process will remain open until 22:00 BST on Wednesday 23rd May. Booking requests made after this time will not be processed.
